Portland Series Snare Drums

Classic Styling

Available in maple and birch shells, our Portland series snare drums feature a minimal set of features, focused on bringing back the classic look from the 60's that so many of us adore.

Goleta Series Snare Drums

Exotic Shells

Designed for the most inspired players, our Goleta series features custom stave, veneer, and solid shell snare drums.

Palmdale Series Snare Drums

Custom Crafted Metal Shells

Our Palmdale series snare drums feature all metal shells, ranging in thickness from 7mm to 1/4 inch. Shells can be beaded or solid-spun, allowing for a broad tuning range and unequaled style.
